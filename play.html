How to play

  • Fill the grid with numbers 1 to 16.
  • The next number must be exactly 1, 2 or 3 squares away.
  • Moves are horizontal or vertical only.
  • No diagonals.
  • Grey shades show the distance to the next number.
  • White = 1 square, light grey = 2, dark grey = 3.
  • The final number is shown in bold.
